# Growth Go-to-Market

Use this skill when the product is ready to be explained, launched, shared, or scaled beyond the internal MVP conversation.

## Primary Job

Turn a working product into a clear market-facing story and a repeatable launch or growth motion.

## Core Rules

- Start from positioning, not from channels.
- Clarify audience, message, and outcome before campaign details.
- Align launch work across product, marketing, sales, and customer-facing teams.
- Think in phases: pre-launch, launch, post-launch.
- Tie growth work to product reality, not marketing abstraction.

## Default Workflow

1. Define who this is for and why it matters now.
2. Clarify:
   - positioning
   - messaging
   - narrative
   - use cases
3. Map the launch or growth motion:
   - pre-launch
   - launch
   - post-launch
4. Identify the smallest next GTM closure:
   - homepage narrative
   - launch article
   - sales or demo framing
   - shareable artifact
5. Sync outputs into:
   - `docs/roadmap.md`
   - growth or launch docs when needed

## Output Standard

Good GTM work should answer:

- who the audience is
- what problem this solves
- why this is different or worth attention
- what action should happen next

## Common Failure Modes

- Jumping into launch tactics before positioning is clear
- Writing copy that sounds good but does not match the actual product
- Treating GTM as a one-day launch instead of a full motion
- Leaving sales, product, and marketing misaligned
